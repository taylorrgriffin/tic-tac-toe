Tic-Tac-Toe Analysis Tool

This interactive module will analyze an in-progess game of tic-tac-toe.

The report will indicate whether the game has been won, whether it's winnable (by either player), as well as some other information.

To begin, click the squares you wish to enter X's or O's in, then click "Run Report." Additionally, you can click "Reset" to clear all squares back to their default values.
